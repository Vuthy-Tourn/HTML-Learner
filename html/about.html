About Me

Hi, I’m Vuthy — a student currently studying Full-Stack development at ISTAD. I really enjoy learning about new technology, and that’s what inspired me to create these HTML Learner. They’re meant to help students improve their coding skills without depending on AI or search engines.


From what I’ve seen, students who try to solve problems on their own tend to build a much stronger understanding of coding. That’s why these challenges focus on critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving — all key skills for becoming a great web developer.


My way of teaching is all about learning by doing. I believe practice is the best way to learn, and these challenges give you a practical way to sharpen your HTML skills through real-world tasks.

Teaching Philosophy

My approach to teaching web development

Learn by Doing

Practical exercises that require active problem-solving create deeper understanding than passive learning.

Embrace Constraints

Limitations foster creativity and force you to truly understand the tools at your disposal.

Build from Fundamentals

Mastering the basics creates a strong foundation for more advanced concepts in frontend.

Encourage Exploration

The best learning happens when students discover solutions through experimentation and critical thinking.
